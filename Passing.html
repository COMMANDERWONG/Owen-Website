Basic skill 3 - Passing

There are 2 kind of passing. Short pass and long pass.
The way of doing short pass is inside leg, this is precise and high success.
The way of doing long pass is outside leg with lock your ankle, you need a good ball feeling to do this action successfully. Remember to lock your led when you kick the ball.
